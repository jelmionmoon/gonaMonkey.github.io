Dem Binaries


Game controls: Drag & drop the binary number to the correct decimal number. The faster you do it, the more points you get!

This is a game of binaries from hell. It was made by three random guys from Finland as a school project.

In this game, you can set a highscore and compete against your friends, making new highscores. You will be given a time of one minute to solve as many binaries as you can!
